Lemon Orzo

Description

This dish is a classic take on an old favorite. Creamy, lemony, and always sure to hit the spot!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 cups of chicken bone broth
  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 2 cups of orzo
  • 1 cup of spinach

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Add your chicken bone broth and bring to a boil
  3. Add the orzo
  4. After 6 minutes, add the heavy cream
  5. As the orzo cooks add the spinach
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
